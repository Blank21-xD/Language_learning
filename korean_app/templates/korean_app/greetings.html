🇰🇷 Essential Survival Greetings

Mastering the basics is the first step to cultural fluency.

안녕하세요
Annyeonghaseyo

Polite Hello. Use this for strangers, teachers, or colleagues.

반갑습니다
Bangapseumnida

Nice to meet you. Used when meeting someone for the first time.

식사하셨어요?
Siksa-hasyeosseoyo?

Have you eaten? A sign of care and well-being in Korean culture.

감사합니다
Gamsahabnida

Thank you (Formal). Always bow slightly when saying this.

죄송합니다
Joesong-hamnida

I am sorry (Formal). Essential for professional social etiquette.

잠시만요
Jamsiman-yo

Just a moment. Useful when asking someone to wait or passing through a crowd.

수고하세요
Sugohaseyo

Keep up the good work. Used when leaving a workplace or ending a task.

잘 먹겠습니다
Jal meok-get-seumnida

I will eat well. Said before starting a meal to show gratitude.
