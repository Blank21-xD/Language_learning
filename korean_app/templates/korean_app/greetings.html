🇰🇷 Essential Survival Greetings

Mastering the basics is the first step to cultural fluency.

안녕하세요 (Annyeonghaseyo)

Polite Hello

Use this for strangers, teachers, or colleagues. It is the safest way to start any conversation.

반갑습니다 (Bangapseumnida)

Nice to meet you

A formal way to express pleasure upon meeting someone for the first time.

식사하셨어요? (Siksa-hasyeosseoyo?)

Have you eaten?

In Korea, this is a sign of care rather than an invitation to a meal. A classic way to check on a friend's well-being.

감사합니다 (Gamsahabnida)

Thank you (Formal)

The standard way to show gratitude. Always bow slightly when saying this to show respect.

Cultural Tip: The Bow

A slight bow while greeting shows respect and humility.
