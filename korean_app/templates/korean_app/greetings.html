🇰🇷 Essential Survival Greetings

Click the cards to reveal cultural context.

안녕하세요
Annyeonghaseyo

Polite Hello. Use this with strangers, teachers, or colleagues.

반갑습니다
Bangapseumnida

Nice to meet you. Used when meeting someone for the first time.

식사하셨어요?
Siksa-hasyeosseoyo?

Have you eaten? A sign of care and well-being in Korean culture.

감사합니다
Gamsahabnida

Thank you. Pair this with a slight bow for maximum respect.
